ABOUT SPACE INVADERS

The game

Space Invaders is a 1978 fixed-screen shooter arcade video game with an alien invasion theme, developed by Tomohiro Nishikado. It was originally produced by Taito, and licensed for production in the U.S. to the Midway Games division of Bally Technologies. Although it is very simple by today's standards, it was one of the most influential video games of its generation: its release effectively decreed the beginning of a period of great fortune for video games, later referred to as the golden age of arcade games. Space Invaders represents one of the best known symbols of the entire video game medium, as well as one of the progenitors of the shooter genre.
